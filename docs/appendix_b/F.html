Grand Vizer Faen¶

Powerful wizard and advisor to Fergus Westwood, he poses a threat to the operations of Acquisitions Incorporated in the Principality of Mingia.

Frat Spectator¶

Encountered in the Tomb of the King of Midnight, the Frat Spectator was found guarding a tomb after a hard night of drinking. Had Eberhard not been able to communicate, this party may have been in for more than they could handle!
