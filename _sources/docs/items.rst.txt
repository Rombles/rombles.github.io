Important Items
###############

The Bone of Doresain
********************

Recovered from the ruins surrounding `The Ouroboros Temple (Original).  <other_locations.html#the-ouroboros-temple-original>`__
This effigy was once the recipient of a great deal of dark magic worship
from long-dead Ouroboros Cult.

The Shards of Incabulos
**********************

An overwhelmingly powerful artifact that is rumored to contain the power of Incabulos himself. Even touching this artifact is enough to tempt them with the sickening power of Incabulos himself.
