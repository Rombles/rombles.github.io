Winsom Landy
~~~~~~~~~~~~

Quest giver that hired 
`HMOB <../franchises.html#hot-mess-of-brie>`__ to escort a found 
magical artifact back to civilization. After accompanything the heroes to 
`The Ouroboros Temple (Original), <../other_locations.html#the-ouroboros-temple-original>`__ 
she rushed ahead of the adventurers and was murdered by
`Calador <C.html#calador>`__ and turned into a ghoul.
